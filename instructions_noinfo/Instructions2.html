Description:

So far, we have only talked about the baseline payoffs you may receive, but the actual payments also depend on the time when you make your decision.

  • Discounting
    • In general, you will earn less the later you make your decision.
    • If you decide at the very beginning of a round while the timer on the left side of the screen still shows sixty seconds, you will receive the baseline payoff.
    • If you decide at a later point in time, you will receive the baseline payoff, multiplied with the Payoff percentage indicated on the right side of the screen. The Payoff percentage decreases as the timer runs.
    • For example, if you choose Heads and your opponent chooses Tails, your baseline payoff is 800 points. With a payoff percentage of 75% at the time when you make your choice, you receive 800⋅75% = 600 points.
    • Once the timer reaches zero, the payoff percentage will typically be 20%, but in one block of the experiment, this value is 33.3%.
    • If you make a decision at some point in time, but the other player does not make a decision before the timer reaches zero, you will earn 500 points times the payoff percentage shown when you clicked the button.
    • If neither player makes a decision, both will receive 500 points times the payoff percentage at the end of the round (i.e., 20% or 33.3%). Hence, 100 or 167 points respectively.

  • Time freezes
    • In general, the timer ticks down continuously, but there is one exception.
    • The timer does not start until five seconds after the beginning of the round. During these five seconds, the “5s time freeze” bar on the left will be active (in green) and the payoff percentage will remain at 100%. If you choose an action during these initial 5 seconds, you obtain your full baseline payoff.

  • End of a round
    • The round finishes once both players have made their decision, or if the timer reaches zero.
    • A “Next button” will appear. Both players have to click their Next button for the experiment to proceed.
    • Please note that both players have to click the Next button for the experiment to proceed.
