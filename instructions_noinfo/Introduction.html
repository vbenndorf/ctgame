On this page, we provide a rough overview of the rules and structure of the experiment. Please read the information carefully. You will need to agree to the rules to proceed with the experiment.

  • Rules
    • Do not use your phone or similar electronic devices during the experiment.
    • Do not talk to the other participants.
    • If you have a question, raise your hand. We will come to you and answer your question. Please do not ask your questions out loud.

  • Structure of the experiment
    • In the beginning, there are 5 training rounds that do not count for your final payoff. Then the actual experiment starts, that consists of 4 blocks. Each block consists of 12 rounds. Including the training rounds, there is a total of 53 rounds.
    • In each round, you are matched with one random other participant.
    • The interactions with the other participants take place in continuous time. That means:
      • Five seconds after the beginning of a new round, a timer will start. You can make your decision within 60 seconds.
      • The round ends once both participants have made their decision, or if the timer reaches zero.
      • In general, your profits will be higher the earlier you make your decision.

We will explain the details of the interaction on the next pages. For now, we would like you to notice the following.

  • Payments
    • In each round you can earn points.
    • The points are added over all rounds (except the 5 training rounds).
    • At the end of the experiment, your total points will be converted to Euros such that 1000 points correspond to 0.7 Euros.
