Thank you for participating in this economic experiment. The research team consists of researchers from Fernuniversität Hagen and from Heinrich-Heine University Düsseldorf, Germany. On the following screens you will find the instructions for the experiment. The experiment will be conducted exactly as described in the instructions.

During the experiment you make decisions on your computer with which you can earn money. What you earn depends on your decisions. Your earnings will be paid to you via SEPA wire transfer or via Paypal a few days after the experiment. You will be asked to enter your payment details at the end of the experiment. Please make sure that you know the E-Mail address associated with your Paypal account or the IBAN and BIC for your bank account.

By participating in the experiment, you consent to the data collected being used for research purposes. All members of the research team will only analyze anonymized data.

Participation in the experiment is voluntary. You can cancel the experiment at any time. In this case, unfortunately, we cannot pay you any money. An early termination will not have a negative impact on your relationship with anybody in the research team or the institutions mentioned above.

By clicking the Next button, you confirm:

  1. that you understand that you have been asked for your consent to participate in the experiment
  2. that you have read and understood the information about the experiment
  3. that you consent to the use of the data you enter for research purposes
  4. that you are participating voluntarily and understand that you can stop the experiment at any time
  5. that you are at least 18 years of age
