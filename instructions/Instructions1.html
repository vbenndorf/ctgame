Description:

The most important information is the Payoff table in the center of the screen. Here you can make your decision and see the baseline payoffs you and the other player can earn if a given cell of the table is reached.

  • How to make a decision
    • In this game, you will select a row of the table and the other player will select a column.
    • You can choose between Heads and Tails by clicking on the corresponding buttons in the row headers (first column of the table).
    • Clicking on the column headers will do nothing.
    • If you decide before the other player, the other player will be informed whether you chose Heads or Tails. If the other player makes a decision before you, you will be informed about it.

  • Interpretation of the baseline payoffs
    • The red entries show your “baseline payoffs”. The black entries are the baseline payoffs of the other player.
    • For example, the upper left cell is reached if you and the other player both choose Heads. In this case, you can earn up to 240 points whereas the other player can earn up to 800 points.
    • The upper right cell is reached if you choose Heads while the other player chooses Tails. In this case, you can earn up to 800 points whereas the other player can earn up to 200 points.
    • Note that there may be subtle changes of the baseline payoffs across rounds.
