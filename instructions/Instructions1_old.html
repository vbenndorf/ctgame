Welcome to this experiment in games in continuous time.

  • General Information:
    • Please plan on staying on your computer for up to 60 minutes.

    • This study consists of {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} of rounds. In each round 2 players compete in a game in continuous time.

    • In each round each player is able to chose between one of two action choices (Heads or Tails) and the timing of their action choice.
